Spider-Man ATSV is a GOATED movie

Spider-Man ATSV Review from imdb

If it wasn't already obvious in the first film, it's now officially clear as day that the people behind the Spider-Verse franchise (including writers/producers Phil Lord and Chris Miller, who are absolute creative godsends to the once magnificent place known as Hollywood) understand EXACTLY what they're working with.

Don't let the marketing fool you: this film has GUTS. It's easily one of the heaviest PG-rated films I've ever seen. Payoffs and character revelations that were only hinted at in the first film are carefully, deliberately unraveled and come around beautifully. It made me think about my own life and what I mean to the people I care about in ways I never expected. The fan-service is BATTY too, and yet somehow it's never too much.I wish every movie was as good as this---but then again if the world was like that this wouldn't feel as special. So I'll just stick to being IMMENSELY grateful that it exists in this universe.This is no ordinary sequel. It's art of the highest form.

My Personal Opinion of ATSV

I agree with this reviewer that Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse is a masterpiece that has captured the hearts of audiences around the world. Directed by Joaquim Dos Santos, Kemp Powers, and Justin K. Thompson, the film was praised for its thrilling action, rich storyline and wide cast of characters. The filmmakers used a unique combination of hand-drawn techniques, computer, and stop-motion animation to create a stunning world that looks like a comic book. As Miles tries to navigate his new powers and responsibilities as Spider-Man, he must face Spider-Man 2099 and other spider people from across the multiverse while dealing with a villain called "The Spot".

Another way Spider-Man:Across the Spider-Verse differs from other superhero movies is the diversity and inclusion of characters. In addition to Miles Morales, this film also includes a series of Spider-Men from various forms, including Gwen Stacy, Peter Parker, and Miguel O'hara. Each character brings their unique perspective and abilities to the story, creating rich characters and experiences that resonate with audiences of all ages and backgrounds. From the creative animation to the riveting storyline and diverse cast of characters, the film offers an unparalleled cinematic experience.
